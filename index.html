About HomeGoods Swap:

Welcome to HomeGoods Swap! Use this site to trade your furniture and decor with your Chicago neighbors to keep your home fresh and exciting. HomeGoods Swap is completely free. Just list the items you want to swap find items you want to have, let your neighbor know you want to make a swap, and see if they are interested in your items. Once you've agreed to swap, you will coordinate a convenient meeting spot and time. Voila! You have a great new item in your home.
